Quantum Field Theory and Combinatorics

University of Waterloo Combinatorics and Optimization (Masters) Waterloo, Canada
Email Scholar ORCID

I’m a first year masters student at the University of Waterloo under the supervision of [Karen Yeats](https://www.math.uwaterloo.ca/~kayeats/). I did my undergrad in mathematical physics at the University of Waterloo. Recently I’ve been working on combinatorial aspects of cosmological correlators and building computational pipelines to study the meromorphic continuation of Euler–Mellin integrals.

I enjoy studying fundamental physics through modern mathematical and computational techniques.

Research Interests

  • Cosmological correlators
  • Euler–Mellin integrals
  • Scattering amplitudes & positive geometry
